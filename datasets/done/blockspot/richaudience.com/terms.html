    1. What are Cookies?

    Cookies are any type of device or similar technologies (such as unique identifiers used to identify an application or device, pixel tags, and local storage) that are placed on a user’s terminal device (computer, Smart TV, mobile phone, tablet, etc.) with the purpose of storing information and retrieving already stored information.

    2. What type of Cookies do we use and for what purpose?

    EXTE’s Websites only use technical Cookies. These Cookies are necessary for the proper functioning of the Websites and cannot be disabled. These Cookies are used to:

    • Ensure safe and uninterrupted browsing across the websites and enable various features and sections.
    • Store user preferences regarding website settings, adapting their experience, and saving their acceptance or rejection of Cookies.
    • Fill out forms or manage a shopping cart.
    • Authenticate or identify the user (session cookies).
    • Enhance user security, such as detecting repeated failed login attempts to a website.
    • Balance website load (session cookies).
    • Customize the user interface and enable certain plug-ins for sharing social media content.
