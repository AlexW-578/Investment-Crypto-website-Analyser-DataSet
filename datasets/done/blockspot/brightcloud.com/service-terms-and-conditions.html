Agreement templates and schedules

Each OpenText™ agreement has a date and version number, generally found in the document footer. If you are a new OpenText customer, the applicable version is the most recent version in the list below. Current OpenText customers should choose the version that relates to the effective date of their purchase. If you are unable to locate the agreement you require, please contact your OpenText Sales representative.

Notice about Application Modernization and Connectivity products and services

On May 1, 2024, Rocket Software acquired all OpenText Application Modernization and Connectivity (AMC) products and services. OpenText is temporarily providing select AMC quotation, invoice, and billing services on behalf of Rocket Software, which will be subject to Rocket Software terms and conditions. Please visit https://www.rocketsoftware.com/company/trust/agreements for more information.
