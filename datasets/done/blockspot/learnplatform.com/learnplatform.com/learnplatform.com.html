Lead with Evidence

With evidence at the forefront of the Every Student Succeeds Act (ESSA), the ability to generate and share valid, rigorous evidence quickly and cost effectively is imperative. LearnPlatform’s rapid-cycle evaluation engine, research team, and shareable badges equip agencies and providers to lead with evidence.

LearnPlatform’s ESSA Evidence Badges™ are quickly becoming a staple for education. Built in consultation with the US Department of Education and its research arm, the Institute of Education Sciences, LearnPlatform’s shareable ESSA Evidence Badges’ and reports enable education organizations and providers to know, show, grow and share trustworthy, valid and ESSA-aligned evidence. 

Look for these badges to easily identify the level and amount of available evidence for any solution.
