WHAT ARE COOKIES?

Cookies are pieces of data that sites and services you use or visit can set on your browser or device and they can typically be read on future visits to learn more about the user and to personalize an experience, including the ads that are displayed to the user. “Cookies” is also a term that may be used to refer to a range of technologies that help an operator identify users and devices, which in turn can help personalize an experience, prevent fraud, store preferences, and speed up the delivery of content.

THE WAY WE USE COOKIES

We set cookies as shown below for a variety of purposes, including:
1) to enable certain functions of the Websites;
2) to authenticate users and prevent fraudulent use of user accounts;
3) to learn more about how Our services are used and similar analytical purposes;
4) to understand how users interact with Our ads;
5) to understand how to improve performance;
6) to customize the Websites;
7) to validate related services; and
8) to provide you with more relevant advertising.

For example, We or Our third-party partners might use a web beacon to learn whether an email message was opened or might store preferences in a flash cookie.

WHAT IS COOKIE REFRESH?

Please note that We use the cookie refresh function in order to refresh the duration period of the cookie set each time you visit the Websites. This means that the expiry term for a cookie is automatically renewed and starts from the beginning in case you visited the Websites again within the initial expiry term of such cookies without disturbing you regarding the additional necessity of giving extra consent for such cookies re-setting. For example, if the refreshment period is 30 (thirty) days and you visit Us every day, this means We will refresh set cookies every day and the whole duration of cookies will constitute the sum up of all refreshments made within your visits.
