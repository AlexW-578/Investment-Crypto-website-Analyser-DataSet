CodeTwo Signatures Web Add-in for Outlook

This modern Web Add-in (co-engineered with Microsoft) adds automatic CodeTwo signatures as you type an email in Outlook on any device (Windows, Mac, OWA, iOS & Android). It can also preview cloud signatures (added after an email is sent). See our quick video

There's nothing to install - just deploy the add-in automatically from the Microsoft 365 admin center to all or selected users in your organization. It only takes a few minutes. Follow these guidelines.
