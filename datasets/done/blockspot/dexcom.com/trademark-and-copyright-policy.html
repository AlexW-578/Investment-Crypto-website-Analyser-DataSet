This document outlines the policy and guidelines of Dexcom, Inc. (“Dexcom”) for the proper use of its trademarks and logos (collectively, the “Dexcom Marks”) and its copyrighted assets (the “Dexcom Copyrights” and, together with the Dexcom Marks, the “Dexcom IP”). The Dexcom IP is owned by Dexcom, which may update this policy from time to time without prior notice.
By using the Dexcom IP, you acknowledge that Dexcom is the sole owner of the Dexcom IP, that you will abide by its guidelines herein, and that you will not interfere with its rights in the Dexcom IP, including by challenging Dexcom’s ownership of, use of, registration of, or applications to register the Dexcom IP anywhere in the world. You also agree that you will not apply to register any of the Dexcom IP—or any marks that are confusingly similar to the Dexcom Marks or content that is substantially similar to the Dexcom Copyrights—in any jurisdiction.
OBJECTIVE AND SCOPE OF THE DEXCOM TRADEMARK & COPYRIGHT POLICY
This document helps ensure that the Dexcom IP is used in approved ways, which, in turn, helps preserve the value of the Dexcom IP and ensure that the Dexcom Marks remain reliable indicators of source and quality. It explains when and how the Dexcom IP may be used and which uses require prior review and authorization from Dexcom.
Dexcom reserves the right to review all uses of the Dexcom IP and to terminate any party’s right to use the Dexcom IP for failure to abide by the terms herein. These terms are general and may be superseded or amended by individual written agreements with Dexcom.
THE DEXCOM IP MAY NOT BE USED IN ANY MANNER OTHER THAN AS EXPRESSLY AUTHORIZED IN A WRITTEN AGREEMENT OR AS SET FORTH BELOW. If any part of this policy conflicts or is inconsistent with any part of a written agreement between you and Dexcom, the written agreement shall control. Unless provided otherwise in a separate written agreement with Dexcom, any use of the Dexcom IP authorized herein is revokable at Dexcom’s option for any or no reason. You agree to comply with any request from Dexcom to stop using the Dexcom IP.

THE DEXCOM WORD MARKS
The Dexcom Word Marks may be used without prior written authorization only to describe Dexcom and/or its products or services, and only if the following are true:
  • The mark is not used to promote any commercial activity or your own products or services;
  • The mark is not used in an attention-getting or branding manner; and
  • The mark is not used in any manner that would create a sense of endorsement, sponsorship, or false association with Dexcom.
Importantly, any such uses must also comply with all other applicable requirements of this policy, including the usage guidelines and prohibitions below. ALL OTHER USES OF THE DEXCOM WORD MARKS REQUIRE WRITTEN PERMISSION FROM DEXCOM.
USAGE GUIDELINES FOR THE DEXCOM WORD MARKS
The following usage guidelines detail how the Dexcom Word Marks must be used. These requirements apply whether or not you have a written agreement with Dexcom.
  • The Dexcom Word Marks must be used as adjectives, never as nouns. This means that they should be followed by a descriptive or generic product/service descriptor—for example, “the Dexcom CGM system.”
  • Never abbreviate, shorten, or alter the mark. Do not pluralize it or make it possessive. When referring to more than one product or service, pluralize the product descriptors instead (i.e., “Dexcom technologies”).
  • The Dexcom Word Marks should never be used in domain names or email addresses, except those owned by Dexcom.
