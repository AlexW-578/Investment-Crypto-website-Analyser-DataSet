Accessibility Policy

Gen Digital Inc. is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all customers and visitors to our websites and users of our mobile applications. Our efforts to improve digital usability and the user experience for everyone, including those with disabilities, are guided by the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 level AA standards and other existing recommendations. We are continually updating our websites and testing accessibility through Siteimprove, a third-party analytics tool.

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of our website and mobile applications. Please let us know if you encounter accessibility barriers by emailing us at accessibility.team@gendigital.com. In your message, please provide your contact information, the web address or other location where you experienced difficulty, and a brief description of the problem you encountered.

About Gen

Gen (NASDAQ: GEN) is a global company dedicated to powering Digital Freedom through its trusted consumer brands including Norton, Avast, LifeLock, MoneyLion and more. Gen brings award-winning products and services in cybersecurity, online privacy, identity protection and financial wellness to nearly 500 million users in more than 150 countries.
