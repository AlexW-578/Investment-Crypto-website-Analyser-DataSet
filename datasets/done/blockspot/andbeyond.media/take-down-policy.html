Take Down Policy

If concerns are raised by any relevant party, including by our own discovery, Our clients or partners, or concerns are raised from consumers, regulators and others, with respect to inappropriate advertisement (ads) or ads appearing on content (inventory) deemed inappropriate, We will make every reasonable effort to take down such ads or placements. SWe will do this by itself, if within our control, or by contacting our partners if not within our control. We will take action as soon as possible, but in any event will start working on the matter within 72 business hours.

This Policy may be amended from time to time by us, if it is deemed necessary in order to reflect our processes and/or to ensure that inappropriate advertisement (ads) or ads appearing on content (inventory) are not being shown or served.
