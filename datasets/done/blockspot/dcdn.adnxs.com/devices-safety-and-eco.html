    Product compliance and sustainability

    At Microsoft, product safety, compliance, and sustainability are integral to our mission to design and build products that empower every person and every organization to achieve more.

    Environmental compliance

    Microsoft devices fulfill strict environmental criteria including, but not limited to, compliance with all applicable legal and regulatory requirements.
    Explore environmental compliance

    Materials and substances

    When we have concern over a chemical’s potential for damage to health or the environment, we believe actions should be taken to gather and assess additional data.
    About materials and substances

    Product safety

    Microsoft cares about the safety of our customers and employees and is committed to product safety. We plan, design, manufacture, sell, and maintain Surface, Xbox, and HoloLens devices to meet or exceed industry standards. Microsoft devices are certified to applicable international safety standards.

    Our Product Safety guidelines describe these commitments and provide additional information about our product safety warnings, product safety alerts, and how to report a potential product safety concern.
    Visit our Product Safety Guidelines

    Batteries

    Microsoft complies with global substance restriction, battery marking, consumer information, and transportation and recycling requirements.
    Explore battery compliance

    Energy

    We work to improve energy-saving features throughout our hardware product portfolio to help our customers save energy.
    Our energy efficiency commitment

    Environmental management system

    We utilize an internationally recognized framework known as ISO 14001 that establishes a process for entities to manage and continuously improve their environmental performance.
    Read about our approach

    Packaging

    Packaging for Microsoft products meet heavy metal restrictions, labelling, and essential requirements regarding packaging optimization, manufacturing, composition, recovery, and reuse.
    Learn about packaging compliance

    Cloud responsible packaging

    We ensure that Microsoft cloud infrastructure hardware packaging meets global requirements and creates minimal environmental impact.
    View cloud responsible packaging

    End-of-life management

    We work with recycling companies and supply chain partners to manage the end-of-life process. Find out where to recycle products, batteries, and packaging.
    Explore our program
