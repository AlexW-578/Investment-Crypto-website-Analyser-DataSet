Why Advertisers Choose Kayzen

Transparency

Gain real-time visibility into every campaign detail with Kayzen. Access comprehensive data on bids, wins, impressions, eCPMs, and more across publishers and exchanges. Enjoy full cost transparency, ensuring complete clarity on your spending and fees.

Control

Take full control of your campaigns with Kayzens self-serve UI. Pull real-time data directly into reporting dashboards or push data into Kayzen instantly through API integrations, empowering you to manage your campaigns with precision and efficiency.

Performance

Achieve superior results with Kayzens optimized bidding models for CPM, CPC, CPI, and CPA. Our modular architecture also supports BYOA (Bring Your Own Algorithm) and BYOS (Bring Your Own Supply), ensuring flexibility and customization beyond the default optimization goals.
