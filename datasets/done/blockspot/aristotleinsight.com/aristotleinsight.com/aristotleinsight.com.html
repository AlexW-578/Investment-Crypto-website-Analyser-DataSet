Aristotleinsight

Providing continuous visibility from the inside out.
Schedule a Demo

Providing you with continuous diagnostics and monitoring for:

Configurations

  • Continuously monitor current configurations and make comparisons against standards including DISA STIGs.
  • Track changes to Active Directory, Permissions, and Policies determine what changed, when the change occurred, and who made the change.
  • View metrics to answer questions such as, Who added a workstation to a domain? and When did this user receive administrative privileges?

Privileged User Management

  • Receive immediate alerts on potentially dangerous activity involving administrative accounts.
  • Discover privilege changes in real time, and maintain an accurate record of actions to protect against breaches from outside and within.

Vulnerabilities

  • Identify risks, direct the remediation process, and document the results.
  • Thoroughly investigate risks with Continuous Diagnostics and Monitoring and maintain a detailed ledger of actions for board meetings and audits.

Asset Inventory

  • Improve your security posture by maintaining an accurate asset inventory of both hardware and software.
  • View which machines didnt correctly receive updates or pose a risk to your organization.

Threat Analytics

  • Protect against insider threats and indicators of attack with real-time alerts.
  • Receive notifications of suspicious activities including USB usage, new processes with network activity, or traffic to blacklisted IPs.

AristotleInsight in Action

Unique Bayesian filtering and data-linking technology allow trends, summaries, and relationships between key security metrics to be shown in the high-level L3 dashboards. These dashboards naturally filter out anomalous activity to alert security professionals of areas needing attention and enable them to quickly pivot into the detailed data to investigate.
AristotleInsights L2 layer presents reports that direct processes and remediation efforts. These reports enable you to identify areas requiring attention, direct efforts to remediate issues, and document the results of what was changed, when it was changed, and who made the changes.
Low-level L1 reports present traditional detailed data for things like logs, user behavior, forensics, and assets. Utilizing AristotleInsights near-instantaneous updating, intuitive filtering, SIEM functionality, and infinite scroll technology, L1 reports allow security professionals to find the detailed data they need quickly.
