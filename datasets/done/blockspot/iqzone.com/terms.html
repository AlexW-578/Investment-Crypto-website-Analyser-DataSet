IQzone Privacy Policy

IQzone provides advertising technology services that allow publishers of mobile applications (“apps”) to show ads in their apps, and for advertisers to reach audiences that may be interested in their products or services. IQzone’s suite of advertising technology includes the IQzone Software Development Kit (IQzone SDK) and the IQzone websites (collectively, the “IQzone Services”).We work with Publisher Partners, Advertising Demand Partners, ane Supported Advertising Mediation Partners (collectively, “IQzone Partners”) in order to provide the IQzone Services. IQzone Partners are each independent controllers of your data.

This Privacy Policy explains how IQzone collects, uses, shares, or otherwise processes personal data.

  • Section 1 describes how we process personal data about you to serve and personalize advertising when you use an app that has integrated the IQzone Services, and how you can exercise control over our processing of your personal data.
  • Section 2 describes how we process personal data about you if you visit iqzone.com.
  • Section 3 describes how we process personal data about you if you are a Publisher Partner or Advertising Demand Partner that uses the IQzone Services.
  • Section 4 covers other important information that applies to all our personal data processing activities, and explains how you can reach us with thoughts or questions about this Privacy Policy.

Section 1: Personal Data We Process to Serve and Personalize Advertising

Personal Data We Collect About You Through Apps

When you use an app that has integrated the IQzone Services, we collect (but do not store) personal data about you. The IQzone Services are designed to avoid collecting information such as your name, address, or email address, but the personal data we collect does enable our partners to recognize your device over time and across apps. Depending on where you live and your privacy choices, the personal data we collect includes:

  • Device identifiers such as your IP address, iOS Identifier for Advertising (IDFA), Android Advertising ID, or an IQzone-specific identifier.
  • Your precise geo-location, when location services have been enabled for an app on your device that has integrated the IQzone Services. Note that when location services have not been enabled in any of your apps that have integrated the IQzone Services, we will still infer data about your location based on your IP address.
  • Information that a Publisher Partner has collected about you and shared with IQzone, such as demographic information (e.g., your age or gender) or information about your interests, to help make the ads served to you more relevant.
  • Information about your device, such as the type and model, manufacturer, operating system (e.g. iOS or Android), carrier name, mobile browser (e.g., Chrome, Safari), and screen size.
  • Information about your app usage, including the apps on your device that use the IQzone Services and the version of such apps, the app on your device that is requesting an ad, the start/stop time of your app session, your current time zone, and your network connection type (e.g., WiFi, cellular).
  • Information about ads served, viewed, or clicked on, such as the type of ad, where the ad was served, whether you clicked on it, and whether you visited the advertiser’s website or downloaded the advertiser’s app.

How We Use the Personal Data We Collect About You Through Apps

We use the personal data we collect about you through apps that have integrated the IQzone Services for the following purposes:

  • Serving Ads: Allowing Publisher Partners to offer advertising inventory in their apps, and Advertising Demand Partners to bid on and fill that inventory with ads.
  • Interest-Based Advertising: Allowing Data Partners and Advertising Demand Partners to infer what you may be interested in and to serve ads to you based on these inferred interests. IQzone’s policies prohibit IQzone Partners from sending, or targeting advertisements based on, information that we consider sensitive or are prohibited by law, such as race, religion, politics, sex life, or health.
  • Geo-Targeting: Allowing IQzone and Advertising Demand Partners to serve ads based on your current or prior location.
    Ad Reporting and Conversions: Reporting to Advertising Demand Partners information about when and how you have been exposed to their ads or clicked on their ads, and reporting to Publisher Partners information about when and how ads were shown on their properties and were clicked on.
  • Frequency Capping: Preventing you from seeing the same ad or type of ad too many times.
  • Providing and Operating Our Services: Auditing, researching, and analyzing data in order to maintain, protect, and improve our Services, and to ensure that our technologies function properly.

How We Share the Personal Data We Collect About You Through Apps

We share personal data that we collect about you through apps that have integrated the IQzone Services for the following purposes:

  • We share personal data with Advertising Demand Partners so that they can decide whether to bid on ad inventory or serve an advertisement and choose the best ad for you across the broader advertising ecosystem. IQzone Advertising Demand Partners are obligated to use the information they receive in compliance with applicable laws and regulations.
  • We share aggregate information with Publisher Partners to help them measure the effectiveness and value of our Services. For example, we provide Publisher Partners with reports about the types of ads served to their apps.
  • We share personal data with IQzone Partners so that they can build and improve their products and services for use on IQzone and across the broader advertising ecosystem. For example, your device identifier may be used by a Fraud and Measurement Partner to improve the fraud detection services that they offer both to IQzone and other advertising companies.
  • We engage service providers to perform functions and provide services to us and IQzone Partners in the United States and other countries. We may share the personal data we collect with such service providers subject to obligations consistent with this Privacy Policy and any other appropriate confidentiality and security measures, and on the condition that they use your information only on our behalf and pursuant to our instructions.
  • We have a written agreement with Intuizi, Inc. (“Intuizi Agreement”) to process data we receive from you for the purpose of verification and device authentication across the internet and to better understand your interests for advertising targeting, research, and/or improving our products and services. Before sending data to Intuizi, we de-identify all of our data by encrypting any information that is linked or can be reasonably be used by Intuizi or a third party to link to an identified or identifiable consumer (e.g. no home addresses, driver’s license or state identification number, passport information, financial account number, login credentials, payment card information, MAIDs, universal IDs, IP addresses, email addresses or other personal data are sent to Intuizi). Our Intuizi Agreement prohibits Intuizi or any third party from attempting to re-identify the sources of our de-identified data. If you assert a right provided by our Privacy Policy or applicable State or Federal regulation to confirm, correct, delete, copy, opt-out, revoke consent or otherwise, we observe that request and our actions immediately and automatically flow to our data processed by Intuizi. Intuizi does not allow personally identifiable information (e.g. personal data) on its platform. To further enhance privacy protections, Intuizi’s platform will not show results derived from de-identified data where the source data could be deemed “sensitive data,” including but not limited to data revealing racial or ethnic origin, genetic or biometric data, religious beliefs, physical or mental health status, including gender-affirming treatments and reproductive or sexual health care; sex life or sexual orientation; status as transgender or non-binary; national origin; citizenship or immigration status; genetic or biometric data; data collected from a known child; and geolocation data linked to an identifiable consumer. Our Intuizi Agreement allows Intuizi to create anonymous data (data that cannot be reasonably linked to an identifiable consumer by anyone, including us) to show on an aggregated basis, including visitation to locations, and application usage. This data cannot be linked to any consumer by any party and Intuizi has contractual and technological limitations to ensure all Intuizi platform data remains anonymous.  Lastly, our Intuizi Agreement requires Intuizi to cooperate with us to comply with our obligations with Federal or State privacy and data protection regulations regarding fulfilling consumer rights requests, ensuring data processing security, and conducting data impact assessments. In some situations, we will share your MAID and/or IP address and/or location data with partners with whom we have written agreements requiring them to comply with the terms of this Privacy Policy and applicable law who will use the shared data for analytics, research and to recognize a device connected to the internet. Data we receive from you may be used to target specific advertising to you while you use apps and websites like Facebook (Meta), Linked-In, Google, Trade Desk and others.

Controlling the Processing of Your Personal Data for Personalized Advertising

We give you control over the types of personal data we collect from you and how we process that personal data to personalize your advertising experience.

European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and Switzerland

Consent: If you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, Publisher Partners who would like us to serve you personalized ads must first obtain your consent so that IQzone and our partners can process your personal data for this purpose. Some Publisher Partners may choose to obtain your consent for this purpose even if you are located outside of these regions. If you provide your consent, we will collect, use, share, and otherwise process all of the personal data described above for the purpose of serving you personalized advertising that IQzone and our partners believe is most relevant to you.

If you decide that you do not want a personalized advertising experience, we will not process your personal data for this purpose. For example, we will not collect, use, or share your iOS Identifier for Advertising or Android Advertising Identifier, your precise location data, or your demographic or interest information. However, we will still serve you contextual advertising — ads targeted to the content or type of app you are using (for example, serving an ad for a video game to a gaming app).


Withdrawal of Consent: You can withdraw your consent to IQzone’s personalized advertising experience at any time.

Publisher Partners may provide a way for you to withdraw your consent directly from within an app. If you withdraw your consent from within an app, we will no longer collect, use, share, or otherwise process personal data from that app to personalize your advertising experience.

In addition, you can use your device settings to let us know that you wish to withdraw your consent to IQzone’s personalized advertising experience across all of your apps on that device.

  • On iOS, you may withdraw your consent by enabling the “Limit Ad Tracking” setting.
  • On Android devices, you may withdraw your consent by enabling the “Opt out of Ads Personalization” setting.

The next time you use an app that has integrated the IQzone Service on that device, we will effectuate your withdrawal of consent, and we will no longer collect, use, share, or otherwise process personal data from that device to personalize your advertising experience.

IQzone does not store your personal data, but we will inform our current IQzone Partners if you withdraw your consent so that they, as independent controllers of your personal data, can also respect your choice to withdraw consent.

Even if you do not consent to a personalized advertising experience, or you later withdraw such consent, we will still process your personal data when necessary to comply with law or to support our legitimate interests, as described below.

Legal Necessity: We will process personal data as necessary to comply with law. For example, we will collect your IP address to perform a lookup of your country so that we know whether you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland. As part of your withdrawal of consent request, we will also make available the iOS Identifier for Advertising or Android Advertising ID of your device to our current IQzone Partners so that they, as independent controllers of your personal data, can also respect your choice to withdraw consent.

Legitimate Interests: We will process personal data as necessary to support our legitimate interests, but we will protect your privacy in doing so. For example, even if you do not consent to a personalized advertising experience, we will still:

  • Receive and send your IP address to our service providers for purposes of detecting invalid clicks and other fraudulent activity on IQzone. We do not retain it internally.
  • Continue to receive personal data from apps that have not been updated to the latest version of the IQzone SDK. In order to provide continuity of our Services to our Publisher Partners and give their users sufficient time to update their apps to versions using the latest SDK, we will continue to support older versions of the SDK. During this time, we will continue to receive personal data such as your iOS Identifier for Advertising or Android Advertising ID from older versions of the SDK; however, in order to protect your privacy, we do not store such personal data.

United States and Other Countries

Opt Out: If you are located in the United States or another country outside the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and Switzerland, you can opt out of receiving personalized advertising by using your applicable device settings:

  • On iOS, you may opt out by enabling the “Limit Ad Tracking” setting.
  • On Android devices, you may opt out by enabling the “Opt out of Ads Personalization” setting.

IQzone requires that our Advertising Demand Partners honor opt-out preferences expressed using these controls by only serving contextual advertisements in response to such ad requests, and prohibits the use of any data contained in such ad requests to serve personalized advertising or to create a profile of the user or device.

Accessing, Restricting, and Rectifying Personal Data We Collect About You Through Apps, and Objecting to Our Use of Such Personal Data

IQzone collects the minimum amount of personal data about you that is necessary to provide the IQzone Services, and does not store any of it. However, as noted above, if you live in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you can exercise control over whether we continue to process your personal data by withdrawing your consent at any time. You can also contact us using the details below.

Privacy Practices of IQzone Partners

IQzone requires IQzone Partners, who are each independent controllers of your personal data, to comply with all applicable laws and regulations, and to provide you with sufficient notice of their processing of your personal data.

When you consent to IQzone’s processing of your personal data, you are also consenting to the processing of your personal data by the IQzone Partners with whom we share your data. If you withdraw your consent (if you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland) or opt-out (if you are located in the United States or another country), we will also inform our current IQzone Partners with whom we have shared your data of your withdrawal or opt-out so that they, as independent controllers of your personal data, can also respect your choice. IQzone Partners may also have separate legal bases for collecting, using, retaining, and sharing your personal data.

Section 2: Personal Data We Process If You Visit iqzone.com

Personal Data We Collect When You Visit iqzone.com

When you visit iqzone.com, our servers automatically record certain personal data in our log files. This web log information includes your IP address, browser type, operating system, the referring web page, pages visited, and cookie information. Like many websites, we use web log information, along with cookies, pixels, and similar technologies, to operate our websites, better understand how they are used, resolve technical problems, and enhance your experience on our sites.

Cookies are small files that websites place on your computer as you browse the web. A pixel is a small amount of code on a web page and is often used in connection with cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies. In many cases you can instruct your browser, by changing its settings, to stop accepting cookies or to prompt you before accepting a cookie from the websites you visit. However, some features of our sites, such as those made available to Publisher Partners and Advertising Demand Partners, may not function properly if you disable cookies.

Personal Data We Share When You Visit iqzone.com

We use a variety of third-party services to help operate and understand the use of our websites. We may share personal data, such as cookie information, with such service providers subject to obligations consistent with this Privacy Policy and any other appropriate confidentiality and security measures.

California Consumer Privacy Act Notice

Last updated: November 25, 2024

This California Consumer Privacy Act Notice explains how we collect, use and disclose personal information relating to California residents covered by the California Consumer Privacy action of 2018 as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act of 2020 (collectively, the “CCPA”).  This notice constitutes our notice at collection and our privacy policy pursuant to the CCPA.  We may change or update this notice from time to time. When we do, we will post the revised notice on this page with a new “Last updated” date.

Introduction

Under the CCPA, “Personal Information” is information that identifies, relates to, or could reasonably be linked directly or indirectly with a particular California resident and includes certain categories of Personal Information discussed below that constitute “Sensitive Personal Information.”

Keeping Personal Information secure is one of our most important priorities. Consistent with our obligations under applicable laws and regulations, we maintain physical, technical, electronic, procedural and organizational safeguards and security measures that are designed to protect personal data against accidental, unlawful, or unauthorized destruction, loss, alteration, disclosure, or access, whether it is processed by us or by others on our behalf.

 

 

1. Data We Collect, Use, Sell or Share

We collect and use personal information about you, and may sell or share that personal information with our partners for the purpose of operating, managing and maintaining our business including to improve our services and/or generate revenue from advertising, analytics, and/or device identification or association. The categories of personal information we collect and use and sell or share are identifiers like your name, alias, online identifier, Internet Protocol address, geolocation, device type, application usage history, transactions, mobile advertising id, persistent identifiers, and inferences drawn from the foregoing to create a profile about a California resident reflecting the preferences, characteristics, and behavior. The categories of sources from which the personal information is collected are mobile phone applications and other applications running on Internet-connected devices. The categories of third parties to whom we may disclose consumers’ personal information are data processors, data analytics services, advertisers, advertising technology providers, marketing technology partners, and advertising agencies.

We may collect sensitive personal information from you. In some circumstances the location data we receive from your device may locate you within a geographic area that is equal to or less than the area of a circle with a radius of 1,850 feet which California defines as “precise geolocation” which is included within California’s definition of “sensitive personal information”.  If we receive geolocation data from your device which falls within the definition of “precise geolocation”, our purpose for receiving geolocation data is to provide you with advertising that may relate to your location, or the authentication of your device, verification, analytics, association of your device to others, and research related to that location.  

We do not retain personal information or sensitive personal information if we have not seen you in a 90 day period.  If we see you in a 90 day period, we will retain your personal information or sensitive personal information until we don’t see you in a 90 day period, or you tell us to delete your personal data.  

We do not sell or share any personal information or sensitive personal information of minors under the age of 16.
