Frequently asked questions

Klaviyos platform is hosted on AWS cloud infrastructure and is a one-to-many, software-as-a-service solution for customers to manage marketing automation and data. This means you fully control and manage your account on the platform and how you configure campaigns, collect and manage data, and send out messages.

Customers use of the platform is governed by our Terms of Use, which incorporates our Data Processing Agreement, Acceptable Use Policy, Privacy Notice, and API Terms of Use (if applicable).

 

Klaviyo is a global company headquartered in Boston, Massachusetts with offices in the US, UK and Australia. The Klaviyo platform and customer data are hosted in AWS data centers located in the US. Klaviyo also uses Sub-processors to assist in providing its services to customers. Information on Sub-processors processing locations are described in https://www.klaviyo.com/legal/subprocessors.

For any additional questions please contact us.
