HMH Policy Framework

Helping all students reach their full potential

We serve all learners and educators

All students deserve the opportunity to reach their full potential. To achieve this goal, HMH works with federal, state, and local lawmakers to advance policies that support teaching and learning.

Our commitments

  • Investing in high-quality resources

    Our materials serve all learners. We believe that equitable access and the tools to drive positive progress are central components of quality.

  • Advocating for effective policies

    Using evidence and expertise, we aim to promote best practices in assessment, curriculum, and professional learning.

  • Supporting educators

    We provide educators with adaptive tools that help them focus their time on improving outcomes.

  • Partnering for high performance

    Through our state and national policy and thought leadership work, we partner with schools and districts to power student growth.

How we fulfill our commitments

We help our federal, state, and district partners transform teaching and learning by focusing on four key areas.

Harnessing tech to impact growth

We provide teachers with products and services that support all students—on one platform. Our formative resources inform personalized learning for every learner. Plus, interim test scores allow educators to check progress throughout the year.

Leveraging data-driven insights

Assessment data helps educators at every level understand whether students’ needs are met and where they require extra support. Using data, we help teachers and leaders understand patterns, focus support, and allocate resources.

Empowering educators and leaders

High-quality resources and assessment data can help teachers and leaders do their jobs, but it isn’t sufficient. Our professional learning incorporates data to support effective teaching and help educators transition to new, more effective materials.

Delivering effective instructional materials

Products and services should reflect the latest evidence of what’s effective in promoting student success. That’s why we help state leaders ensure access to research-backed, relevant materials in every classroom.
