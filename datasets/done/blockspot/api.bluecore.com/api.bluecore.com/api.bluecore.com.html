Creating a profitable customer base requires customer-led technology.

Old World
New World

The channel-led strategy is characterized by low retention and customer survival rates – average 22% 3-year retention rate, and more than 2x lower than leading brands. This eats into a retailer’s growth and compounds over time. 

Teams are operating in channel silos with a short supply of signal to drive customer movement through the lifecycle and end up over-investing in bottom-funnel performance to hit year-over-year comps.

Retailers and DTC brands who shift to customer-led technology are driving higher retention and long-term growth.

Retailers Bluecore retailers see an average 59% 2-year retention rate — more than 2x higher than lagging brands.

This approach is built on real-time shopper identification, customer signals, and product insights, seamlessly activated across channels and conversations. The result? Hitting comps, lower costs, and a more engaged, profitable customer base.

Build an agent that speaks your brand’s language

Configure your AI agent with your brand’s personality and deploy custom skills to guide shoppers from discovery to conversion.
Retail Skills

Like an in-store associate, your AI agent needs the right tools to help shoppers. Use our pre-built skills, or create your own, to deliver personalized guidance.

Our pre-built skills include AI-generated prompts for styling, product comparisons, review summaries, and cart building.

Still, no one knows your shoppers better than you. Our developer and marketer-friendly solution lets you build and customize skills tailored to your brand.

Guidance & Control

AI is evolving fast, but you stay in control. Set instructions, transparency, and brand values at the core of every interaction. Define your agent’s role, tone, and limitations to align with your brand.

Monitor conversations in real time and create custom rules to guide responses. Keep customer and business data protected with built-in privacy and compliance safeguards.

Build trust at every touchpoint — your agent shows where information comes from, helping shoppers make confident decisions.
