What is emltrk.com?

Our popular Email Analytics tool offers email designers advanced open and read statistics for their email campaigns. In order to offer this data, we ask our users to embed a small snippet of HTML in their email, emltrk.com is included within this HTML.

Spam

Litmus explicitly forbids any of our users from using Email Analytics for subscribers who have not opted-in. Our Terms of Use (which all users must agree to) state:

Under no circumstances may you use our tools in connection with the sending of Unsolicited Commercial Messages (spam). This includes our Email Analytics tools. You warrant that all your subscribers are opt-in and that every message you send has an appropriate mechanism for unsubscribing from your mailing list.

Our full terms of use

What to do if you've received spam

If you've received an email containing emltrk.com that you believe is unsolicited, please let us know immediately by forwarding the email to emltrk-abuse@litmus.com. Our support team will investigate within 24 hours. If we discover the sender of the email is abusing the emltrk.com domain, we'll disable that user's account and blacklist them from using our products and services in future.

Questions and concerns

If you have any other questions or concerns, we'd love to hear from you! You can reach us at hello@litmus.com or by calling us on +1 (866) 787-7030. Our support team typically answer emails within 24 hours.
