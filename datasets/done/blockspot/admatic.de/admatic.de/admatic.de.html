Becoma a Publisher

How can I generate revenue with SSP?

Admatic SSP connects your ad inventory with global advertisers through real-time auctions (RTB). Our Header Bidding technology ensures that you earn maximum revenue by displaying the highest-paying ads.

What is Header Bidding, and how does it work?

Header Bidding is a technology that allows you to present ad auctions simultaneously to multiple ad networks. This ensures you receive the highest bid for every ad impression, optimizing your revenue.

What ad formats are supported on the platform?

Our platform supports a wide range of ad formats, including video, display, native, and mobile app ads.

How is my data protected?

Admatic prioritizes data security and is fully compliant with global data protection standards such as GDPR and CCPA. All user and platform data is processed securely.

How long does it take to set up and integrate the platform?

The setup and integration of SSP are quick and efficient. Our technical support team will guide you through every step of the process, and most publishers can complete integration within a few days.

How can I manage my ad campaigns with DSP?

Admatic DSP allows you to manage your campaigns with AI-powered targeting and optimization tools. You can track performance in real-time and make the most of your budget.

On which channels can I run my ads?

Admatic offers the ability to run campaigns across various channels, including display, video, mobile, and native ads. You can also leverage programmatic auctions to connect with premium publishers.

What targeting options are available?

Our platform provides advanced targeting options, such as demographic, behavioral, geographic, and contextual targeting, making it easier to reach the right audience.

How can I monitor my ad performance?

With Admatic DSPs user-friendly dashboard, you can monitor your campaign performance in real-time. Detailed analytics help you make data-driven decisions to enhance your ROI.

Can I advertise with a minimum budget on Admatic?

Yes, Admatic DSP offers flexible solutions for different budgets. You can start with a small budget and scale up based on your campaigns performance.
