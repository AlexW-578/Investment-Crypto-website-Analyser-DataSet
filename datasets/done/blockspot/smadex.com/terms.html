Cookies Policy

The purpose of this Cookies Policy is to help you understand how the website of the company, “SMADEX S.L.U” (hereinafter, “SMADEX”) uses cookies (or similar technologies), their purpose, as well as the options that you, as a user, have at your disposal to manage them.

DEFINITION AND FUNCTIONS OF COOKIES

A cookie is a file that is downloaded to your computer, tablet, mobile or other terminal (hereinafter, equipment) when you access the website: www.smadex.com (hereinafter, “the Site”).

In general, and depending on the type, cookies allow to store and retrieve information about your visit, your browsing habits, the browser or device from which you access, as well as to recognize you as a user or customer, which can facilitate your next visit and make the site more useful by customizing its content. Cookies play a very important role in improving the experience of using websites.

Cookies can be “first-party” cookies, which are those that are sent to your computer from a domain or computer managed by SMADEX; or they can be “third-party” cookies, which are sent to your computer from a computer or domain that is managed by another entity.

They may also be “session” cookies, which means that they expire when you leave the Site; or “persistent” cookies, which remain stored on your computer for a longer period of time, ranging from a few minutes to several years.

WHAT TYPE OF COOKIES WE USE? 

  • Technical cookies: They are necessary for the correct development of the Site and the different options or services that exist in it, such as, for example, controlling traffic and data communication, identifying the session, accessing restricted access parts or using security elements during the navigation, among others.
  • Personalization cookies: These allow the user to access the service with some predefined general characteristics based on a series of criteria in the user’s terminal, such as language.
  • Analysis cookies: These allow you to track and analyze the behavior of the users of the Site (e.g. number of pages you visit, the place from which you access, the frequency and recidivism of visits, the duration of your visit, the browser or the equipment from which you visit, etc.). The information obtained is used in measuring the activity of our website and for the elaboration of user navigation profiles, with the purpose of introducing improvements based on the analysis of the use data made by users of the website.

AUTHORIZATION, REVOCATION AND DISPOSAL

When browsing the Site for the first time, you have been informed about the use of cookies, of the possibility of accepting their installation, or rejecting the installation of certain types of cookies, such as analysis, advertising and cookies from third parties.

Likewise, and at any time, you can allow, block or delete the cookies installed on your computer by configuring the browser options or from the privacy options of the computer from which you are visiting us. In the event that you block the installation of technical cookies in your browser, you may not be able to access certain sections of the Site and/or use some of the services offered.

If, through the initial configuration, you had accepted the installation of certain types of cookies and subsequently wanted to oppose their installation or maintenance, you must delete the cookies already installed through the configuration of the browser options, and reconfigure those of which you allow its installation
