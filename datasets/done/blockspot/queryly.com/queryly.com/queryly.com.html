Search

Queryly brings your audience the most compelling search experience on the web. It makes searching on your site fast, relevant and visually appealing. You also have options to promote your own video and content as part of results.

  1. Instantly Fast Search API
    Queryly brings a "search as you type" experience to your audience. it is lightning fast and delivers instant gratification of content discovery at your fingertips, literally.
  2. Aesthetics and Custom Search Design
    Queryly displays results beautifully. When people enjoy the search experience, they will search more and discover more.
  3. Relevant Results
    Queryly's semantic engine processes every single article and extracts key concepts and knowledge to make search results more relevant.
  4. Real Time Search Analytics
    People communicate their interests and demands through search. Queryly's dashboard helps you better understand what your users are looking for in real time.
Ask

Unlock the full potential of AI with Queryly Ask  a solution built to provide fast, intelligent, and privacy-centric responses. Queryly Ask ensures every answer is rooted in your content, enhancing relevancy while minimizing hallucinations.

  1. Relevant answers with citations
    Say goodbye to vague or unreliable AI responses. Queryly Ask delivers accurate answers backed by citations from your trusted content. This ensures users not only get the information they need but also the confidence of knowing where it comes from.
  2. Built-in Chatbot and Ask API
    Connect with your audience in new ways through our intuitive chatbot interface. Plus, the Ask API enables seamless integration into your existing systems, providing you with the tools to enhance your applications with new AI capabilities.
  3. On-Prem solution for privacy
    Your data is your greatest asset. Our On-Prem deployment ensures your sensitive information stays within your environment.
Discovery

Queryly Discovery is a smart recommendation service that generates related stories for every single article on the site. It takes an algorithmic approach and analyzes every single word in every article to determine relevance. It requires no human intervention and improves itself over time as more people use it.

  1. Relevant
    By leveraging our semantic engine and users' inputs, Discovery brings truly relevant recommendations based on each article to keep audience more engaged.
  2. Recommendation Widgets
    Their UX and behavior are fully customizable with standard HTML, JavaScript and CSS.
  3. API support
    Developers can build more engaging applications on top of Discovery API.
