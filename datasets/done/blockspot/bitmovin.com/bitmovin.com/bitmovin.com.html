Cloud Native

Reduce operating expense

Stream video in the highest quality while cutting costs with Bitmovin’s cloud-native solutions. The VOD Encoder optimizes file sizes and accelerates content processing, saving up to 50% on storage and bandwidth, while the Live Encoder ensures 24/7 broadcast-grade reliability and resiliency for events and linear channels. With dedicated SDKs, the Bitmovin Player guarantees seamless playback on all devices, reducing maintenance costs. Analytics provides deep insights to enhance performance and minimize downtime. Together, these products boost efficiency, automate processes, and lower your total cost of ownership. Plus, with Cloud Connect, you can use existing cloud credits in your private instances on major cloud providers, further minimizing costs.

Ad Insertion

Generate new revenue

Unlock new revenue streams with Bitmovin’s monetization capabilities, supporting AVOD, SVOD, and hybrid monetization workflows. Seamlessly integrate dynamic ads with support for both Server-Side Ad Insertion (SSAI) and Client-Side Ad Insertion (CSAI) for on-demand content, and utilize SCTE-35 markers for precise ad insertion during live broadcasts, turning your streams into profitable opportunities. Enhance ad playback with a dedicated ad module that keeps viewers engaged while gaining insights into ad performance, helping you maximize advertising opportunities and significantly boost revenue.

Flawless Video

Retain and grow subscribers

Stream unparalleled viewing experiences with Bitmovin to retain and grow your subscriber base. Deliver flawless video while supporting the latest codecs for high-quality streaming, even in lower bandwidth environments. Provide a superior viewing experience across any screen with support for every major device, and gain insights into viewer behavior to analyze each session, boosting engagement and ensuring your audience keeps coming back.
