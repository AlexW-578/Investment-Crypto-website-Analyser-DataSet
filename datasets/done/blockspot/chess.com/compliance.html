Compliance With The European Union Digital Services Act

CHESScom
Updated: Jan 1, 2025, 6:25 AM | 64 | Other

Reporting on January 1, 2025.

In compliance with the REGULATION (EU) 2022/2065 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 19 October 2022 on a Single Market For Digital Services and amending Directive 2000/31/EC (Digital Services Act), we are reporting here on the average monthly active users for each of the platforms of Chess.com and related businesses. 

To calculate the average monthly active users, we analyzed the number of individuals in the European Union that have actively used our platform in each 6-month period, regardless of if they are registered users or not. Once we gathered all the information for each month, we calculated the average. You can see the average for each of our platforms below:

  • Chess.com: 7,863,503
  • ChessKid: 75,424
  • Dr. Wolf: 35,577
