What to expect

Purpose-built for higher ed course evaluations

More responses, more impact

Higher response rates yield more reliable, inclusive results. Ensure students complete their surveys through reminders, alerts, and optional final grade gating.

Meet students where they are

Watermark Course Evaluations & Surveys lets students respond via smartphone or directly through any major LMS like Blackboard, Canvas, and D2L.

Simplify administration

Enjoy minimal IT involvement and painless implementation. Integrations with your SIS and LMS take minutes, not hours, so you can build on your existing technology right away.

Add dimension to your data

Build and tailor your evaluations with ease. Start with your campus-wide survey and add questions for specific areas or courses. Instructors can add questions too!

Get immediate results

Conduct surveys anytime to gauge performance. With immediate insights, instructors can refine course material and teaching methods to impact current students, not just future ones.

Rich reporting without a learning curve

Give faculty and administrators reporting that helps them synthesize, aggregate, disaggregate, look longitudinally, draw comparisons, and distill takeaways – without intensive training.

Built-in qualitative analysis

Help faculty glean course-based insights from students’ free-text responses faster with AI-powered sentiment analysis.

Faculty development

Surface student perspectives in faculty reviews to evaluate an instructor’s classroom effectiveness. Assign evaluation reports to Watermark Faculty Success profiles seamlessly.

Drive continuous improvement

Inspire curricular and program enhancements by including students’ feedback in your annual assessment cycles. The connection to Watermark Planning & Self-Study makes this easy.

Go beyond course evaluations

Augment your understanding of your stakeholders. Use surveys to more deeply understand the experience of your students, faculty, staff, and alumni.

Part of Watermark’s EIS

Course Evaluations & Surveys is just one part of our Educational Impact Suite. This suite of complementary solutions, connected by our Insights Hub, uncovers key insights to help you drive progress.
