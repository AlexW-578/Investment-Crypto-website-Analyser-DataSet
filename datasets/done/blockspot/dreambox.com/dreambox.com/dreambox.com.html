FAQs

How does DreamBox Math support student growth?

Whether learners need intervention, remediation, or enrichment, DreamBox Math and English meet them where they are and keep them growing!

How does DreamBox Math personalize learning?

DreamBox Math and English combine an engaging curriculum with Intelligent Adaptive Learning™ to provide dynamic, motivating experiences tailored to each child’s unique needs.

How does the adaptive technology work?

DreamBox’s adaptive software evaluates childrens’ interactions and adjusts and readjusts in real time, so every learner can progress at a pace and on an instructional path that’s right for them.

Is DreamBox backed by research?

Rated STRONG by Evidence for ESSA and validated by third-party studies, DreamBox has demonstrated efficacy across every achievement level, in every learning environment and with a variety of learners.
